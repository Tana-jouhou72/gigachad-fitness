### How to Test:

#### Method 1: Browser Developer Tools
1. Open your website in Chrome/Firefox/Safari
2. Press F12 (or Ctrl+Shift+I)
3. Click the mobile/tablet icon
4. Select different devices (iPhone, iPad, etc.)
5. Test navigation and interactions

#### Method 2: Real Mobile Device
1. Open your website on your phone
2. Test all navigation links
3. Check if text is readable without zooming
4. Verify buttons are easy to tap

#### Method 3: Online Mobile Testing
1. Use tools like:
   - Google Mobile-Friendly Test
   - BrowserStack
   - Responsinator.com
