- Go to repository Settings
- Scroll to "Pages" section
- Source: "Deploy from a branch"
- Branch: "main"
- Folder: "/ (root)"
- Click "Save"

### 4. Access Your Live Website
- Wait 2-5 minutes
- Visit: https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/gigachad-fitness
- Your website is now live!
